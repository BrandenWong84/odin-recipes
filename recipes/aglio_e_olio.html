Aglio e Olio

A Brief Description...

Aglio e olio is Italian for "Garlic and Oil", which speaks to the simplicity of the dish. It only requires 3 ingredients: Garlic, Oil, and Pasta.

Ingredients for Aglio e Olio

  • Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • OPTIONAL: Red Pepper Flakes

Steps

  1. Cook pasta in a pot of boiling water
  2. Add salt to the water
  3. Separately, cut garlic into thin slices
  4. When pasta is a few minutes away from finished, start heating a pan on a low temperature
    1. Low can be different depending on equipment. For my gas stove, I set the burner at a 3 out of 10.
  5. Add oil to the pan
  6. When oil is hot, add garlic
  7. Once garlic is fragrant, put finished pasta into the pan
  8. Stir and toss pasta until liquid is no longer runny
  9. Add red pepper flakes if desired
  10. Serve and enjoy!
