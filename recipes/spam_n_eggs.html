Spam n' Eggs Recipe

A Brief Description...

Spam and eggs are a simple and easy Hawaiian classic.

Ingredients for Spam n' Eggs

  • Spam
  • Eggs
  • OPTIONAL:Rice or bread

Steps

  1. Heat up a small pan on medium
  2. Slice spam into desired shape
  3. When pan is hot, add spam to pan
  4. Cook spam until brown
  5. Remove spam and set aside, leaving the rendered fat in the pan
  6. Cook eggs any way that you desire
  7. Scrambled is a classic, but I prefer a gooey fried egg or sunny side up
  8. When egg is cooked, plate with spam
  9. OPTIONAL: Plate over rice
  10. Serve and enjoy!
